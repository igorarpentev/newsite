Game news

Black Friday (November 25, 2022)
Black Friday is coming - it's time for discounts, which will not bypass our game.

In the garage you will be pleased with:
— Discounts on kits 25%
— Discounts on paints 50%

Also, as compensation for recent failures in the operation of game servers, the probability of gold boxes falling out has been increased fourfold, a premium account has been added to all players for two days so that during the holiday discounts you can accumulate crystals and spend them with pleasure!

And these are not all nice bonuses. Yes, yes, we finally realized that it was a complete failure to allow a situation in the game when there is an imb gun without resists, as much as for a whole month, but it's better late than never — with the update, hammer-proof paints and new kits appeared in the game.
Now you don't have to wait for the list of kits to be updated, you can buy any kit available in the garage at any time.
25.10.2022
Update (November 25, 2022)
Twins:
— The speed and range of the projectile has been reduced by 7%.
Flamethrower:
— Damage at the maximum range of defeat has been reduced from 50% to 30%.
— The recharge rate has been increased by about 10%.
— Power consumption reduced by 10%.
Isis:
— The "Double Energy" reserve no longer increases the power of "vampirism".
— The original range of the gun that it had when it was released has been returned. That is, the range of Isis is increased by 2 units on each modification.
Frieze:
— Damage at the maximum range of defeat has been reduced from 50% to 30%.
— Recharge reduced by about 10%.
Rebound:
— The energy consumption per shot is reduced by about 7-8%.
Shaft:
— The minimum damage in aiming mode has been increased by about 40%.
— The maximum damage in aiming mode has been increased by about 8%.
— Offhand damage increased by about 40%.
— Energy consumption when shooting offhand is reduced by 33%.
— The rotation speed has been increased by 5%.
— Rotation acceleration increased by 20%.
— Added a beam that other players can see while the shaft is in aiming mode;
— Added a shot through tanks, similar to the "Rail" cannon, but having a value of 50% for all modifications.

Added paint with hammer gun protection.
